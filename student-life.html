Guilford College

Student Life

Guilford College offers a vibrant and diverse student life experience, with a wide range of clubs, organizations, and activities for students to get involved in.

Clubs and Organizations

Guilford College has over 50 clubs and organizations, including academic clubs, cultural clubs, service organizations, and special interest groups.

Student Government

Guilford's Student Government Association (SGA) is the voice of the student body and works to represent student interests and concerns on campus.

Events and Activities

Guilford's campus is home to a number of cultural and social events, including concerts, lectures, and art exhibits, providing students with a rich and dynamic college experience.
